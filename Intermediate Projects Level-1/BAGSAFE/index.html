Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about ExamSafe

How do I find storage near my exam center?
Simply enter your exam center location in our search bar, and we'll show you all available homes offering storage services in that area. You can filter by distance, price, and available facilities.
When do I get the homeowner's contact details?
After you complete the booking process and payment, you'll receive the homeowner's full contact information and exact address. This ensures a secure transaction for both parties.
What safety measures are in place?
All homeowners are verified through our screening process. We require identity verification and only list homes near recognized exam centers. Additionally, our rating system helps you choose the most reliable options.
Can I cancel my booking?
Yes, you can cancel up to 24 hours before your exam for a full refund. Cancellations within 24 hours may be subject to a fee as specified in our cancellation policy.
